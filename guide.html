Check-in Instructions

Arrive at the property

Head to Tennyson Street, Petone. Look for the warm, modern home with the dark roof.

Park in the driveway

Pull into the driveway on the left-hand side. Additional parking is on the street.

Approach the front door

Walk up the path to the front door. You'll see the Yale smart lock keypad.

Enter your access code

Type your personal access code on the keypad and press the check button. The door will unlock.

Make yourself at home

Step inside, close the door behind you, and settle in. Everything you need is here.

  • No smoking inside the property (outdoor area is fine)

  • Quiet hours after 10:00 PM — please be considerate of neighbours

If your code doesn't work, make sure you press the check button after entering all digits. If it still doesn't open, text or call James immediately.

The remote is on the wall in the living room. Press the power button, set to HEAT mode, and adjust to your preferred temperature (20–22°C is comfortable).

Turn on the TV with the Samsung remote. Netflix, Disney+, and Apple TV are preloaded. Please don't log out of any accounts.
