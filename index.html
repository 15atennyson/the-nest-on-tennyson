About this home

Welcome to The Nest on Tennyson — a fully self-contained Wellington accommodation nestled in one of Petone's most charming streets. This beautifully renovated, warm and modern 3-bedroom, 2-bathroom retreat is perfect for families, groups, and anyone wanting to explore the Wellington region from a comfortable base.

The open-plan living and dining area flows through French doors onto a private courtyard with pergola — ideal for morning coffee or evening drinks. The fully equipped kitchen features quality appliances including a gas range, dishwasher, and large fridge-freezer. Three generous bedrooms provide comfortable sleeping for up to 6 guests (4 adults and 2 children), including a dedicated kids' room with bunk beds.

Located on a quiet residential street in Petone, Lower Hutt, you're just a short stroll from Jackson Street's renowned cafes and boutiques, Petone Beach, and the railway station with direct trains to Wellington CBD. The property includes a double garage and off-street parking. Whether you're visiting for a weekend, a week, or a month, our family-friendly holiday home is your ideal base.

Location

Petone is a vibrant waterfront suburb in Lower Hutt, at the northern end of Wellington Harbour. Known for its eclectic mix of cafes, boutiques, and weekend markets along Jackson Street, it offers the perfect balance of local village charm and easy access to the capital — making it one of the best locations for accommodation in the Wellington region.
